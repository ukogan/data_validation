Benefits of This Approach
  • Standby Emphasis: Right side (green) standby percentages are prominent and easy to scan
  • Visual Balance: Well-balanced systems show symmetrical patterns
  • Immediate Correlation Assessment: Misaligned bars instantly show correlation problems
  • Space Efficiency: Single chart replaces two separate correlation charts
  • Intuitive Reading: Left=occupied, Right=standby mirrors natural reading patterns
  • Pattern Recognition: System health becomes visual pattern matching
